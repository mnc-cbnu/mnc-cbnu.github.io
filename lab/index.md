<h1>MNC Lab (Mobile Networking & Computing Lab)</h1>

<p>
Our lab focuses on next-generation mobile networking systems including
Internet of Vehicles (IoV), Content-Centric Networking (CCN), and Edge Computing.
We aim to design scalable, intelligent, and efficient communication systems.
</p>

<hr>

<h2>Research Areas</h2>
<ul>
  <li>Vehicular Networks & IoV</li>
  <li>Content Caching & Delivery</li>
  <li>Edge Computing Systems</li>
  <li>AI-driven Network Optimization</li>
</ul>

<hr>

<h2>Facilities</h2>
<ul>
  <li>Vehicular mobility simulation testbed</li>
  <li>Network protocol emulation platform</li>
</ul>
